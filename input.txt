A chatbot is a computer program that simulates human conversation with an end user. 

An organization has many advantages of using chatbots for business growth, process efficiency and cost reduction.

Rule-based chatbots and AI-Powered chatbots are the common types of chatbots.

Examples of chatbots: Google Assistant, Amazon Alexa, Apple Siri,etc.

An AI, or Artificial Intelligence, refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and mimic human actions.
